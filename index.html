4-Step Posture Correction System

Medical Note: Consult a healthcare professional before starting if you have existing spinal conditions or chronic pain.

1. Posture Awareness Training

Develop body awareness with these techniques:

  • Wall Test: Stand with heels 6" from wall - head, shoulders and buttocks should touch simultaneously
  • Mirror Checks: Perform quick posture checks every 30 minutes when working
  • Posture Apps: Use apps like Posture Reminder or Upright for real-time feedback

2. Essential Posture-Strengthening Exercises

These physical therapist-approved moves target key postural muscles:

  • Chin Tucks: 3 sets of 10 reps daily to correct forward head posture
  • Scapular Squeezes: Hold for 5 seconds, 15 reps to strengthen upper back
  • Thoracic Extensions: Over foam roller to improve spinal mobility

3. Workplace Ergonomics Optimization

Set up your workspace using OSHA ergonomic standards:

  • Monitor Height: Top at eye level (use stands if needed)
  • Keyboard Position: Elbows at 90-110° with wrists straight
  • Chair Settings: Lumbar support with hips slightly higher than knees

4. Daily Mobility Routine

Incorporate these into your morning/night routine:

  • Chest Opener: 30-second doorway stretch each side
  • Spinal Decompression: 1-minute hanging from pull-up bar
  • Hip Flexor Stretch: 45 seconds per side to prevent anterior pelvic tilt
